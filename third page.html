Juliet Rose

The Juliet rose is a peach-colored rose that symbolizes love, passion, and devotion. It's a popular choice for weddings and anniversaries.

  1. Appearance

    • The Juliet rose has a cup-shaped bloom with many overlapping petals.

    • The petals are arranged in multiple layers.

    • The rose has a soft peach color that graduates throughout the petals
